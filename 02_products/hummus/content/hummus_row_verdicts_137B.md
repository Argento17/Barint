  whole beans), the thing that distinguishes *this* tub from the cluster.
- **S2 — the honest verdict:** what earns the score and what holds it back, in plain terms.
- **S3 (optional) — position:** where it sits on the shelf / who it's for. Use only when it adds
  signal; a quiet 2-sentence verdict is correct for a typical product.

Rules: no per-grade boilerplate ("פרופיל הרכב טוב…"), no banned phrases, numbers only from the
trace, restrained but not hedged. The "best ≠ excellent" framing is allowed for the A product.
