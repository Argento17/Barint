**Scope:** Hebrew market only  
**Coverage:** 5 live categories + future category candidates  
**Last updated:** 2026-05-31  
**Owner:** Marketing Agent  
**Use for:** On-page SEO, meta copy, content topic selection, newsletter subject lines

---

## How to Read This Document

**Tiers:**
- **Tier 1 — Head terms:** High volume, high competition. We want to appear here eventually, but ranking requires domain authority. Use in page titles and H1s.
- **Tier 2 — Mid-tail:** Moderate volume, moderate competition. Primary ranking targets for existing comparison pages.
- **Tier 3 — Long-tail:** Lower volume, high purchase intent. Easiest to rank for now. Use in prologue text, articles, newsletter.

**Intent codes:**
- `[CMP]` — Comparison intent (user wants to compare options)
- `[INF]` — Informational intent (user wants to learn)
- `[PRD]` — Product-specific (user is researching a named brand or product)
- `[CNCN]` — Concern-based (user has a specific ingredient or health concern)

**Hebrew search note:** Israeli search queries frequently omit definite articles (ה) and prepositions that would appear in written Hebrew. Keyword forms below reflect common search forms, not grammatically perfect Hebrew.

## Keyword Implementation Guide

### Meta Titles — Pattern

`השוואת [category] — [year] | ברי`

Examples:
- `השוואת יוגורטים — 2026 | ברי`
- `השוואת חטיפי בריאות — 2026 | ברי`
- `השוואת מעדנים — 2026 | ברי`

**Rule:** Update the year in January each year. Keep max 60 characters including brand.

### Meta Descriptions — Pattern

`[specific claim about what Bari did]. [Call to discovery].`

Examples:
- `בדקנו 45 יוגורטים ישראלים לפי הרכב הרכיבים שלהם. גלה איזה יוגורט עומד בפני הרכיבים שלו.`
- `השוואנו 53 חטיפי בריאות ישראלים. לא כל מה שקרוי "בריאות" הוא בריאות.`

**Rule:** Max 155 characters. Always include a specific number (53 products, 45 yogurts). Always end with action/discovery language.

### H1 Tags — Pattern

Category name in Hebrew, plain, above the fold. No taglines in H1.

Examples:
- `יוגורטים — השוואה`
- `חטיפי בריאות — השוואה`
- `מעדנים — השוואה`

### Prologue Text — Keyword Integration

The CategoryPrologue (2–3 sentences) should naturally contain:
- The category name in Hebrew (2–3x)
- "השוואה" or "בדקנו" at least once
- One specific data point (number of products, a surprising finding)
- No keyword stuffing — write for the reader, not the crawler

### Newsletter Subject Lines — Keyword-Adjacent

Subject lines should mirror Tier 3 long-tail queries in natural Hebrew:

- "מה יש בתוך [common product]?" — mirrors `[INF]` intent
- "31 מתוך 45 [products] מכילים X" — mirrors `[CNCN]` intent + specificity
- "איזה [category] מנצח?" — mirrors `[CMP]` intent
