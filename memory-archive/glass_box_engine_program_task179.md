**Glass Box (TASK-179)** = owner-initiated program to evolve BSIP2 from a single collapsed grade into **six explicit, separable dimensions** while PRESERVING one decisive consumer grade: D1 nutrition (TASK-169 rubric) · D2 ingredient evidence · D3 processing signal (**de-moralized** — population-level probabilistic, not a verdict) · D4 additive evidence (**the moat — evidence-tiered: confirmed-negative/likely-neutral/functional/dose-dependent/contested/disclosure-gap**) · D5 transparency/disclosure (**NEW, scored — "we score what you disclose, flag what you hide"**) · D6 confidence (extend `INSUFFICIENT→null`, can demote/withhold grade). Headline grade = f(D1–D5) gated by D6; reframed internally as *within-shelf ranking over observable data, not a health verdict*. Two surfaces: consumer (grade+confidence+drilldown) vs professional (full graph).
