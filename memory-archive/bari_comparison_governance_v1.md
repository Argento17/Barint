## Key Rules to Remember

- Score delta ≤2 = noise → declare equivalent, do not rank
- Score delta 3–6 under partial confidence → declare equivalent
- Purpose divergence statement is MANDATORY before score, not after
- "Effectively equivalent" is a valid and intended comparison output
- No ranking under absolute purpose divergence, insufficient data, or active unresolved distortion
- The Category Launch Approval Checklist is a hard gate — not advisory
