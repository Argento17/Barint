**Why:** Bari needs cross-category editorial consistency before expanding to new categories. This is the infrastructure layer.

**How to apply:** All future editorial content, card copy, badge wording, and category rollouts reference this document. It is the source of truth for approved language, forbidden phrases, transparency wording, and archetype definitions.

## Seven Components

1. **Expectation vs Reality Library** — 7 terms (מחמצת, כוסמין, מלא, עשיר בסיבים, דגנים, חלבון, ללא סוכר): consumer assumption, Bari validation, bread dataset evidence, suggested consumer wording
2. **Editorial Language Standard** — Approved phrases (Hebrew), forbidden phrases with replacements, 4 tone rules, claim boundary definitions
3. **Insight Card System** — 5 card types: Expectation Gap, Surprising Mainstream, Transparency, Mechanism Explainer, Category Pattern. Each has title length, body length, tone, structure, evidence style, CTA style + bread example
4. **Consumer Translation Layer** — 30+ examples across 5 dimensions (fermentation, grain structure, fiber source, seeds, processing/confidence) in 3 lengths: short (badge), medium (card), tooltip
5. **Transparency Doctrine** — When Bari refuses to score, 3 data tiers (verified/partial/insufficient), uncertainty wording table, conservative interpretation rule, evidence thresholds per signal, retailer limitation wording, 3-level disclaimer hierarchy
6. **Product Archetype System** — 6 archetypes: Honest Simple, Halo Product, Structurally Strong, Ambiguous Wellness, Transparency Failure, Mechanism Mismatch. Each has definition, signals, consumer wording, tone, overclaim risks, bread examples
7. **Next Category Recommendation** — Ranked 5: Yogurt (#1), Cereals/Granola (#2), Milk Alternatives (#3), Protein Bars (#4), Oils/Dressings (#5)

## Key Design Decisions

- **Forbidden words include:** רעיל, מזויף, מסוכן, בריא, לא בריא, נקי, מלוכלך, הכי טוב בישראל, אמיתי/לא אמיתי
- **Conservative interpretation rule:** When ingredient is ambiguous (e.g., כוסמין without מלא), assign less credit, not more
- **Transparency = editorial posture, not apology:** "46% ללא ציון — כי אין נתונים" is a finding, not a failure
- **Archetypes reuse across categories:** Halo Product, Mechanism Mismatch, Transparency Failure all directly transfer to yogurt, cereals
